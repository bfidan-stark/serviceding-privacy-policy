1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how two-run it-solutions GmbH (“the Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, processes, stores, and protects personal data obtained through the Service-Ding mobile and web application.

Service-Ding is a service management platform that enables restaurants and cafés to organize and operate a waiter call system efficiently.

2. Data Collected

The following types of personal data may be collected from users:

  • Business type (e.g., restaurant, café, bakery, etc.)
  • Business name
  • Business location
  • Business or personal phone number
  • Email address
  • Business floor names
  • Business working days
  • Business opening and closing hours

3. Purposes of Data Processing

Personal data collected from users is processed for the following purposes:

  • Identity verification and account creation
  • Providing user support and communication
  • Managing user accounts and system functions
  • Sending verification codes via SMS or email
  • Delivering notifications and system messages

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

User data may be shared only for the purpose of operating and maintaining the Service-Ding application with third-party service providers such as:

  • OpenRemote (data management infrastructure)
  • SMS provider (for verification code delivery)
  • Cloud and hosting service providers (for system operation and maintenance)

These third parties are authorized to process personal data solely for technical or operational purposes and are not permitted to use it for marketing or other unrelated activities.

5. Data Retention Period

User data is retained as long as the account remains active. Once the account is deleted, personal data will be securely stored for an additional 15 days for technical and security reasons, after which it will be permanently deleted from all systems.

6. Data Security

All personal data is stored on secure servers located within Germany and the European Union (EU). Appropriate technical and organizational measures, including SSL encryption, access control, and authorization mechanisms, are implemented to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or misuse.

7. User Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and KVKK, users have the following rights:

  • To access their personal data
  • To request rectification of inaccurate data
  • To request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • To restrict or object to data processing
  • To request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, users may contact us directly at office@two-run.com

8. Cookies

Currently, the Service-Ding application does not use cookies. If cookies are implemented in the future, explicit consent will be obtained from users, and a detailed Cookie Policy will be made available.

9. Emails and Notifications

The application may send emails or in-app notifications for system messages, verification codes, or promotional announcements. Users provide explicit consent to receive such communications and may withdraw this consent at any time.

10. Underage Users

Service-Ding may be used by individuals under the age of 18. However, users are responsible for the accuracy of the information provided and for obtaining any necessary parental or legal consent when required.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will become effective once published within the application or on the official website. Users will be notified of significant updates where required by law.

12. Contact Information

For any inquiries, complaints, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact: office@two-run.com
